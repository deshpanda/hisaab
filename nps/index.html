    Accumulation uses the SIP convention (contribution at the start of each month, compounding monthly). The pension is pre-tax — annuity income is taxed at your slab. Rules per the PFRDA (Exits & Withdrawals) Amendment Regulations, 2025.

    The exit rules this page applies (non-government subscribers)

    • Since December 2025: at normal exit you may take up to 80% as lump sum; at least 20% must buy an annuity. (The earlier rule was 60/40.)
    • Tax hasn't caught up: only 60% of the corpus is tax-free as lump sum. Withdraw 80% and the extra 20 points are taxed at your slab — the calculator shows that split explicitly.
    • Small corpus: up to ₹8 lakh at exit can be withdrawn 100%.
    • Normal exit now triggers at age 60 or 15 years of subscription, whichever is earlier; phased withdrawals (SLW) can run to age 85.
    • Deductions: the extra ₹50,000 under 80CCD(1B) is old-regime only; in the new regime the employer's NPS contribution (up to 14% of Basic+DA) is the deductible route.
